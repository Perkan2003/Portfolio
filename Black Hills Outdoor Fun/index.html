ATV TOUR INFO

All of our tours are approximately 4 hours in length. There is no need to worry about getting lost or reading maps. Competitive ATV businesses in the area rent you the ATV, give you a map and say good luck. We want to do things a little different here at Black Hills Outdoor Fun and give you a worry free FUN experience to let you enjoy the scenery and the riding without the worry of time schedules, getting lost, or running out of gas.

The tour we are on gives you beautiful majestic views of the Black Hills. The trails accommodate everyone from beginners to experienced riders. There are some areas of rocks and and a few mud holes, depending on the weather conditions but most of the trails are on Forest Service trails.

WHAT TO BRING

When you are riding in the Black Hills, you should always keep in mind that the weather can change in an instant. Here is our suggested list of what to bring for your ATV ride:

  • Sunglasses or some sort of eye protection. Goggles (non-tinted) are available with the rental
  • Waterproof and breathable rain gear
  • Extra Clothing: Dressing in layers is wise but also remember to bring extra clothing if it turns cold.
  • Sunscreen: While it may feel cool in the mountains, the UV rays are much more intense at higher elevations and you will burn faster.
  • Backpack or other type of pack to secure your personal items, including your phone or camera. Trails and ATV’s can be bouncy and we don’t want you to loose or damage your phone if it was not properly secured.
  • Shoes should have closed toes. Sandals are not recommended.
  • Valid Drivers License.

Water and snacks are provided by Black Hills Outdoor Fun. Groups can bring food or drinks that they wish however NO ALCOHOL OF ANY KIND IS ALLOWED!! The ATV’s do have racks on them and we can bungie cord your belongings to them.
